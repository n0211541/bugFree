About Me

My name is Teresa Horton. I began working for Liberty as a contractor in July 2011 and was hired in November of 2012. I worked as a Personal Rater in Production while I earned my Computer Science Degree from SNHU. After that I was promoted to a Business Analyst on the CTI Team. I have worked in this position for three years.

On a personal level, I live in Berwick, ME. with my husband of 26 years. We have five children and two beautiful little granddaughters ages two and three years.

Past Experiences

In the past I have run a business and a church. At one point I worked for a draft horse supply place as a customer service rep. While there, I created their tack/book/farrier catalogs. Later I took a position doing investigations in the fraud unit at the National Visa Center.

In my spare time, I have run a children's ministry with 30 volunteers underneath me. I really enjoyed this position which entailed curriculum development, volunteer training, scheduling and planning special events such as Trunk or Treat.
